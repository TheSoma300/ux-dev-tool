The Roadmap

Methodology

Agile Scrum is going to be used as a software delivery methodology, with 2 week sprints, and retrospective, refinement and planning ceremonies in each sprint. Daily stand-ups are going to be held with specific stakeholders to the current task.

PM

A Product Manager is needed to be assigned to overlook the roadmap and give support where the engineers' hands are tighed. Advised to have a PM present on daily stand-ups.

UX

UX will be a crucial stakeholder in the project, in terms of design, functionality requirements and scope of the project. It is advised to have a representative present on daily stand-ups.

DevOps

DevOps team is required for the release definition and ecosystem environment set up. Certain sprints will require a representative for refinements.

DOX

DOX teams inputs are needed for documentation and language presented for the tool.
